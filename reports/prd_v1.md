# Product Requirements Document (PRD): RuralRoots AI - Phase 1 MVP

## 1. Product Overview
RuralRoots AI is an AI-powered marketplace and logistics platform that connects rural artisans and agricultural producers directly to urban consumers ("Rural-to-Urban Artisanal E-commerce").

### Target Audience:
- **Artisans/Sellers:** Small-town producers with high-quality goods but low digital literacy.
- **Urban Consumers:** Conscious shoppers (Gen Z/Millennials) seeking authentic, traceable, and direct-to-source products.
- **Logistics Partners:** Local fulfillment hubs and transport networks.

---

## 2. Core Features (MVP)

### A. AI-First Onboarding (Seller Side)
- **Voice-to-Listing:** Artisans can record a voice note describing their product. AI generates the title, SEO description, and fills in category/tags.
- **Image-to-Market:** AI analyzes photos of handmade goods to suggest pricing based on similar 2026 market trends.

### B. "Source-to-Table" Traceability (Consumer Side)
- **Live-Streaming Integration:** Dynamic video feeds for "Live Commerce" (e.g., watching a weaver work).
- **QR-Code Provenance:** Digital certificates of authenticity and origin.

### C. Agentic Logistics Engine (Back-of-House)
- **Route Optimization:** AI-driven grouping of small shipments to reduce "Last-Mile" costs by 20%.
- **Demand Forecasting:** Predicts seasonal surges in rural clusters to pre-place inventory in mini-hubs.

---

## 3. Tech Stack Requirements (Draft)
- **Frontend:** Next.js with localized language support (multilingual voice-first UI).
- **Backend:** AI Core (OpenAI/Gemini) for text/image analysis + MCP-based logistics tracking.
- **Database:** Pydantic-based state management with PostgreSQL for order tracking.
- **Logistics API:** Integration with local hub APIs + AI route-graph optimization.

---

## 4. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Avg. Onboarding Time:** < 5 minutes for non-tech-savvy sellers.
- **Logistics Margin Recovery:** Minimum 15% reduction in shipping overhead.
- **Retention:** 40% repeat buyer rate within 6 months.

---

## 5. Roadmap
1. **Phase 1 (MVP-A):** Voice-first listing & basic marketplace.
2. **Phase 2 (Scalability):** Integrated Agentic Logistics & Hubs.
3. **Phase 3 (Expansion):** Global cross-border artisanal export.
