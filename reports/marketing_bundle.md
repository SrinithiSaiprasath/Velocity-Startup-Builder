# Marketer Agent: Growth & Branding Strategy

## 1. Brand Identity: "RuralRoots AI"
- **Tagline:** "The Heart of the Village, Direct to Your Table."
- **Brand Essence:** Authenticity, Technology, Empowerment.
- **Tone:** Transparent, Community-Driven, Efficient.

## 2. Marketing Bundle (Phase 1 MVP)

### Social Media Campaign: "Meet the Maker"
- **Instagram/TikTok:** 15s-30s "Day in the Life" reels of artisans. AI-generated captions in the buyer's language.
- **X (Twitter):** Weekly "Supply Chain Transparency" threads showing how many hands a product *avoided* vs a standard retailer.

### Performance Marketing:
- **Google Search:** Targeting "Conscious Consumerism," "Direct-to-Farmer," "Artisanal Gifts 2026."
- **Meta (TikTok):** Ad creative focusing on the "Live-Stream" aspect: "The farmer just harvested this honey. Is it yours yet?"

### Community Strategy:
- **The RuralRoots Collective:** A loyalty program for early-adopter buyers who provide direct feedback to the artisans.
- **Referral Program:** "Gift a Gift with a Heart" - incentivizing users to share artisan stories.

---

## 3. Launch Timeline (90 Days)
- **Day 1-30:** Recruiting the first 20 artisans (The "Alpha Pioneers"). Filming content.
- **Day 31-60:** Alpha launch on social media. Influencer outreach (Eco-friendly/Tech-forward).
- **Day 61-90:** Official "RuralRoots Expo" - A live commerce event with all 20 artisans participating.
