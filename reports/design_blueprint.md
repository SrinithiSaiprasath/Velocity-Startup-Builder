# Designer Agent: UX/UI Blueprint for RuralRoots AI

## 1. Visual Language
- **Palette:** Earthy tones (Terracotta, Sage Green, Ochre) mixed with modern "Tech-Sleek" UI highlights.
- **Topography:** Bold, accessible fonts with high-contrast icons for low-literacy users.
- **Imagery:** Focus on High-Res 4K textures of handmade goods and genuine artisan portraits.

---

## 2. Core User Flows
### Flow A: The "One-Tap Video Listing" (Seller Perspective)
1. **Home Screen:** Large "Record Video" button.
2. **Recording:** 15s video clip with voice description.
3. **AI Post-Processing:** Automated split of Video to:
   - High-Quality Product Photos
   - Short Reels for Marketing
   - Voice-to-Text Listing (Product Name, Price, Category)
4. **Final Confirmation:** Seller taps a large "Checkmark" icon.

### Flow B: The "Provenance Discovery" (Buyer Perspective)
1. **Product Page:** Dynamic Storytelling Banner (Artisan's Bio + Location).
2. **Traceability Card:** Interactive map showing the path from Rural Hub to the buyer's home.
3. **Instant "Live-Chat":** Direct 1-on-1 video or text chat with the artisan (AI-translated in real-time).
